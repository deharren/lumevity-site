How It Works

Learn. Practice. Certify.

A structured path from first concept to documented competency — designed to complement your clinical placement, lab, or workplace training.

1

Learn

Work through video demonstrations and step-by-step interactive modules that explain the anatomy, evidence, and technique behind each procedure.

2

Practice

Use printable competency checklists and guided practice plans to rehearse skills in the lab or under qualified supervision until they feel second nature.

3

Certify

Pass module knowledge checks to earn a verifiable certificate of completion you can share with instructors, employers, or in your portfolio.

About the Founder

Taught by a clinician who does this every day

Lumevity was founded by Lacey Smith, a Certified Family Nurse Practitioner with twelve years of healthcare experience and the owner of IV Wellness in Cincinnati. She has trained and mentored countless students and new clinicians on the very skills these courses teach.

She built Lumevity to give the next generation of healthcare workers a clear, confidence-building way to learn procedural skills — combining real clinical expertise with patient, plain-language teaching, so learners arrive at the bedside prepared and safe.

— Lacey Smith, FNP-C
